Bexley Senior
Tag

Bexley Senior Tag is a yearly tradition where seniors in high school form teams and play tag against each other with squirt guns. During my senior year of high school, I created a website that would track the game. The website had a feed that showed “kills” in real time, player information, and the overall scoreboard. The website was built using PHP and MySQL. This project was my first exposure to databases.
